The rules
←
Drag left: decimal moves right past digits. Each step adds 1 to exponent.
→
Drag right: decimal moves left. Each step subtracts 1 from exponent.
✓
Stop when exactly one non-zero digit is left of the gold dot.
n
The exponent equals how many places you moved the decimal.
Example: 5,900 → slide left 3 places → 5.9 × 10³
The gold dot started after the 5900, moved 3 positions left.
