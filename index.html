The rules
left
Drag left: decimal moves right. Each step adds 1 to exponent.
right
Drag right: decimal moves left. Each step subtracts 1 from exponent.
stop
Stop when exactly one non-zero digit is left of the decimal.
n
Exponent = how many places you moved the decimal.
Example: 5,900 — slide left 3 places — 5.9 x 10^3
