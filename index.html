Scientific Notation

Why write 1.5 × 10¹¹ instead of 150,000,000,000? The exponent carries the meaning — and makes comparison effortless.

The zoom trick: zooming from 10⁰ (person) to 10¹¹ (Earth-Sun) takes 11 full steps — that gap is 100,000,000,000 times. Scientific notation makes it just a subtraction: 11 − 0 = 11. The exponent IS the number of steps.
